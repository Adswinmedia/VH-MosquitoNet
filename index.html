FAQ

Common Questions

Find answers to the most frequently asked questions about our services

How long does installation take?
Most installations are completed within 2-4 hours depending on the size and complexity. For larger properties or multiple installations, we may require up to a full day. We offer same-day installation for urgent requirements.

What warranty do you provide?
We provide a comprehensive warranty on both materials and workmanship. Mosquito nets come with 2-3 years warranty, while bird nets come with 3-5 years warranty depending on the material grade selected.

Are the materials safe for children and pets?
Absolutely! All our materials are 100% safe, non-toxic, and eco-friendly. We use certified materials that are completely safe for children and pets. The nets are also fire-resistant and weather-proof.

Do you offer custom sizes?
Yes! All our installations are custom-made to fit your exact measurements. We measure your windows, doors, or balconies during the site visit and create nets that fit perfectly without any gaps.

How do I maintain the nets?
Maintenance is very simple! Just wipe the nets with a damp cloth monthly to remove dust. For deeper cleaning, you can use mild soap and water. Avoid harsh chemicals. The aluminum frames are rust-proof and require no maintenance.
